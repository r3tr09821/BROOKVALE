2 Children Missing Near Circus

Police are investigating the disappearance of 2 children last seen near the NEVER LAND Circus grounds.

Authorities have launched a search operation after two children were reported missing in the vicinity of the recently reopened NEVER LAND Circus. Witnesses claim the children were last seen near the circus entrance late last night. Police have cordoned off the area and are interviewing staff and visitors.

The incident has sparked concern among local residents, especially given the circus's mysterious history and recent reopening. Anyone with information is urged to contact the police tip line immediately.
